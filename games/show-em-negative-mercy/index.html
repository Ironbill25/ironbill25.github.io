Uno: Show 'Em Negative Mercy


Play UNO No Mercy Scratch / TurboWarp version (Show 'Em Negative Mercy), a fan-made UNO game based on Show 'Em No Mercy.


This game is often searched as "UNO No Mercy Scratch", "UNO No Mercy TurboWarp", and "Show 'Em No Mercy online".

This game was made with Scratch and TurboWarp. You can use TurboWarp for better performance. Stacking cards is allowed in this game!

UNO No Mercy (Negative Mercy) Rules

These are the house rules for Show 'Em Negative Mercy:

All original rules from UNO: Show 'Em No Mercy apply, but there's also a new rule:
Absolutely No Mercy Rule: Instead of being kicked out when you have 25+ cards, it takes 50 cards! Extra brutal.

Cards

This game includes all of the cards from Uno: Show 'Em No Mercy, and a few extra:

  • Wild Draw 16 - The next player must draw 16 cards, or stack another Wild Draw 16. Stack this a few times, then the first player that can't stack it loses...
  • Wild Reverse Draw 6 - Reverses the direction of play, then the next player must draw 6 cards, or stack another draw card.
