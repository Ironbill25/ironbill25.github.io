Uno: Show 'Em Negative Mercy


Play UNO Show 'Em Negative Mercy on Scratch/TurboWarp, a fan-made UNO game based on UNO: Show 'Em No Mercy.


This game was made with Scratch and TurboWarp. You can use TurboWarp for better performance. Stacking cards is allowed in this game!

UNO Negative Mercy Rules

These are the house rules for Show 'Em Negative Mercy:

All original rules from UNO: Show 'Em No Mercy apply, but there's also a new rule:
Absolutely No Mercy Rule: Instead of being kicked out when you have 25+ cards, it takes 50 cards! Extra brutal.

Cards

This game includes all of the cards from Uno: Show 'Em No Mercy, and a few extra:

  • Wild Draw 16 - The next player must draw 16 cards, or stack another Wild Draw 16. Stack this a few times, then the first player that can't stack it loses...
  • Wild Reverse Draw 6 - Reverses the direction of play, then the next player must draw 6 cards, or stack another draw card.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this an official game?

No, Show 'Em Negative Mercy is a fan-made game, not an official UNO game.

Why was Negative Mercy created?

Negative Mercy was created because I just felt that No Mercy just wasn't brutal enough. You've probably seen those memes with people having over 60 cards in their hand, this is one of those games where that's possible.

Can I play this online or with friends?

Yes, you can play this online on the TurboWarp version. However, if you use the Scratch version, you'll have to be signed into Scratch (and not be a New Scratcher) to play multiplayer. You'll also want a decent internet connection. However, we don't currently have local multiplayer.

What is TurboWarp?

TurboWarp is a faster version of Scratch that compiles projects into JavaScript, allowing them to run much faster than they do on Scratch. It is recommended to play on TurboWarp if you have access to it.

Is there an app I can download?

Currently there is not an app for Negative Mercy, however most browsers like Chrome let you save the page as a web app.

It looks like there was an error loading the page, how do I fix this?

This probably happened because some filter blocked TurboWarp. Try clicking the "Switch to Scratch version" button. Please note that the Scratch version does not have online multiplayer unless you log into Scratch.
