Uno: Show 'Em Negative Mercy


Welcome to Uno: Show 'Em Negative Mercy! This version of Uno is based off Uno: Show 'Em No Mercy, but with a few changes.
Stacking cards is allowed in this game!

Rules

All original rules from No Mercy apply, but there's also a new rule:
Absolutely No Mercy Rule: Instead of being kicked out when you have 25+ cards, it takes 50 cards! Extra brutal.

Cards

This game includes all of the cards from Uno: Show 'Em No Mercy, and a few extra:

  • Wild Draw 16 - The next player must draw 16 cards, or stack another Wild Draw 16.
  • Wild Reverse Draw 4 - Reverses the direction of play, then the next player must draw 4 cards, or stack another draw card.
